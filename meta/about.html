This site is a home for news of all sorts. Some of it isn't true. Hopefully all of it is fun, pretty, thought-provoking, or a combination of those things. It is produced at an organic pace. A slow pace, if you will.


If you've enjoyed what you've seen so far, send an email to bless@sindicatonews.com. Ask - in the nicest way you know how - to be notified when new content comes about. When content drops, a shoddy email will be delivered to your inbox.


Everything here is made by folks working to improve some creative skill, writing or otherwise. Your feedback, big or small, constructive or complimentary, is quite appreciated. Check the feedback page for more details.


Thanks for reading and supporting.
